Design Principles

Proximity — Kaden Johnson

The proximity is really good here because it it easy to read and you know exactly where to
look and you aren't confused on what is going on.

Alignment — Ryan Gibb

The alignment is basically perfect here. The picture and the navigatoin is lined up correctly
and the little blue protruding part catches your eye so you will look there and hopefully do
what it wants you to.

Repetition — Dane Kitchens

The same color is being used throughout the whole website. There isn't any confusion if you
ended up on a different website.

Contrast — Kaden Johnson

The contrast between the picture and words is extreme making it easy to read and catches
your eye.

Typography — Dane Kitchens

There are a few fonts but nothing is confusing. I'm glad they didn't use the font on the
pictures for the navigation.

Site Purpose Statement

We want people to know excatly where they are at. We want our mobile website to work
flawlessly and with ease.
